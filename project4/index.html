Welcome!

Welcome to Love Notes and Pens. We are a small stationary business based in Baltimore, Maryland. We started our journey in 2016, selling decorative pens, notepads and notebooks in a pop-up shop at the downtown harbor. Our shops gained a lot of sales and we we're able to create our online store front, which is where you are now. We hope you find something you like, thank you for visiting!
